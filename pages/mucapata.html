Ingredients you will need

  • 1kg of rice
  • 500 g of soroco beans
  • 3 coconuts
  • 1 liter of water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

How to prepare Mucapata?

  1. Wash the rice and hulled soybeans.
  2. Both are put to cook in a pan with a liter of water and salt, for twenty minutes.
  3. The coconuts are grated; heat a little water (warm) and squeeze the milk.
  4. Add the milk to the previous preparation.
  5. Leave on the heat until the beans are cooked and the sauce is dry.
  6. It's ready to serve.
  7. It can be served with grilled chicken or cabidela rice.
  8. This dish is best cooked in a clay pot.
